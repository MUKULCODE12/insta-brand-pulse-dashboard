Insta Brand Pulse — Key Insights
🟦 PAGE 1: EXECUTIVE OVERVIEW INSIGHTS
📌 Insight 1: Engagement ≠ Follower Size

Large brands do not always achieve the highest engagement levels.
👉 Some mid-sized brands outperform bigger brands in engagement.

📌 Insight 2: Growth varies across brands

Certain brands show significantly higher growth rates, indicating effective content and audience strategy.

📌 Insight 3: Category-level differences exist

Different industries (Beauty, Electronics, etc.) show varying engagement and growth patterns.

🟩 PAGE 2: CONTENT STRATEGY INSIGHTS
📌 Insight 4: Reels dominate engagement

Brands with a higher percentage of reels content consistently achieve higher engagement rates.

📌 Insight 5: Balanced content performs better

Brands using a mix of reels, carousel, and static content show more stable performance.

📌 Insight 6: Static content underperforms

Static posts generally have lower engagement compared to reels and carousel content.

📌 Insight 7: Content strategy drives performance

Engagement is more influenced by content type than follower count.

🟨 PAGE 3: BUSINESS IMPACT INSIGHTS
📌 Insight 8: Trust drives growth

Brands with higher trust scores tend to experience stronger follower growth.

📌 Insight 9: Engagement ≠ Sales

High engagement does not always translate into high sales impact.

👉 Important business takeaway

📌 Insight 10: Brand recall boosts discovery

Brands with higher recall scores are more easily discovered on Instagram.

📌 Insight 11: Brand strength is multi-dimensional

A brand may perform well in engagement but not in trust or sales, indicating the need for balanced strategy.
